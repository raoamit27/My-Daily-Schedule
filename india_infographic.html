The Legal Toolkit

A suite of stringent laws is frequently deployed against critics. These laws are often characterized by broad definitions and severe restrictions on bail, making the legal process itself a form of punishment.

Process as Punishment

The Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act (UAPA) has an extremely low conviction rate. This suggests its primary use is for prolonged pre-trial detention to silence individuals, rather than to secure convictions.
